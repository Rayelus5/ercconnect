$1K to Start Building the Project, Launch of the New Token

Posted by

Rayelus

CEO and Founder, ERC Connect

Today, we’re happy to announce our Series 0 funding. This is a major milestone for our company, customers, and community in our mission to build our project, together.

A big push comes for the ERC Connect startup, beginning with the launch of its new token on the Solana blockchain.

We are working hard to be able to launch our ERC Connect services to the whole community, but as any serious project needs an initial investment, we are currently in this situation. We started by launching a series of applications within the ERC Connect ecosystem, and a trial token on the Polygon network. None of these services and apps were automated.

Our entire ecosystem is open source, and all developments are announced on the El Rincón Crypto Community (ERC) Discord server.

ERC Connect is a project designed for the community to support their favorite creator in a different and unique way, through the blockchain and the consumption of entertainment applications within the ecosystem.

Thanks to the annual subscription memberships and test phase support it is hoped that it can help with the initial investment of the project. We don't mince words, every project needs an initial investment. From our side we will invest more than $1K including all previously raised.

ERC Connect is an exclusive project for its community, and is not expected to be released to the general public. It is the community itself that should benefit. Thanks to the support we receive from users we can identify who are the ones who have supported or support the channel the most, something we take into account when rewarding the community with airdrops, quality content on Youtube, and of course, giveaways.

The new $ERCC token will allow users to support the creator by purchasing such tokens and add liquidity to the creator.

Our mission within El Rincón Crypto is that you can learn new things, get detailed guides about airdrops where you can earn thousands of dollars or even receive valuable knowledge about cryptocurrencies. And ERC Connect makes sure that all users who want to support the YouTube channel and the community itself can do it in a much more entertaining way, investing in the ecosystem services.

We remain focused on helping everyone to build a greater community.

Details of our Series 0

ERC Connect will add an initial investment of $1K or more, including with the participation of the community itself and its founders to get the project off on the right foot, starting with its tokenization with the launch of the new token $ERCC and development of Web 3.0 applications.
